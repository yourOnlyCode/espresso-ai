# DocAuto - Business Strategy & Implementation

## Executive Summary

DocAuto is a SaaS document automation platform specifically designed for financial services learning & development teams. Built to address the unique compliance, training, and policy documentation needs of mid-market banks, credit unions, and wealth management firms.

## Market Opportunity

### Target Market
- **Primary:** Regional banks and credit unions in Charlotte, NC area
- **Secondary:** Telecom L&D departments (leveraging your domain expertise)
- **Expansion:** Insurance, healthcare, and other regulated industries

### Market Size
- Mid-market financial services: 5,000+ institutions in US
- Average 100-200 employees per institution
- $25-50/user/month = $2,500-10,000 MRR per customer
- TAM: $250M+ annually

### Competitive Advantage
1. **Vertical Focus:** Built specifically for financial services compliance
2. **Local Presence:** Charlotte banking hub proximity
3. **Domain Expertise:** Your L&D + telecom background
4. **Compliance-First:** Pre-built templates for SOX, FINRA, GDPR
5. **AI-Powered:** Intelligent document analysis and generation
